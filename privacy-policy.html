Discord Moderation Bot

Privacy Policy (UK – GDPR Aligned)

Last Updated & Effective: April 2026

1. Data Controller

The Developer of this Bot is the data controller under the UK GDPR and the Data Protection Act 2018. Contact details are provided in Section 12.

2. Data Collected

We collect and process the following categories of personal data (via the Discord API and only as necessary for the Service):

  • Discord User IDs and usernames (pseudonymous identifiers).
  • Server (Guild) IDs and channel IDs.
  • Message content, metadata, and attachments solely when required for moderation, logging, or enforcement of administrator-configured rules.
  • Interaction data (e.g., commands issued, automated actions triggered).

We do not collect sensitive special-category data unless incidentally present in moderated messages (in which case it is processed only as required for moderation).

3. Legal Basis for Processing

Processing is based on legitimate interests (UK GDPR Article 6(1)(f)): providing effective server moderation and safety tools at the request of server administrators. Where message content is processed, this is limited to what is strictly necessary (data minimisation).

4. Automated Decision-Making and Profiling

The Service uses automated systems to apply moderation actions based on administrator-defined rules (keyword detection, behaviour thresholds, spam patterns, etc.).

These decisions may produce legal or similarly significant effects (e.g., temporary/permanent bans).

Meaningful information about the logic involved: Rules are pre-configured by server administrators and applied consistently and transparently. The Bot does not use AI/ML profiling beyond rule-based triggers unless explicitly enabled and documented by the administrator.

Safeguards (UK GDPR Article 22):

  • Users have the right to request human review of any automated decision.
  • Users may contest or appeal the outcome via the server’s designated moderation process.
  • Server administrators are responsible for overseeing and intervening in decisions where appropriate.

We do not make solely automated decisions outside the scope of administrator-configured rules.

5. Data Usage

Data is used exclusively to deliver the moderation features requested by server administrators. We do not use data for marketing, profiling for advertising, or any unrelated purpose.

6. Data Retention

Data is retained only as long as necessary for the provision of moderation services (e.g., logs are typically kept for 7–30 days or as configured by the administrator). Data is automatically deleted when: (a) the Bot is removed from the server, (b) a deletion request is honoured, or (c) it is no longer required for legitimate moderation purposes.

7. Data Sharing and Transfers

We do not sell data. Data is not shared with third parties except:

  • With Discord Inc./Discord Netherlands BV as required by the Discord API and Developer Terms of Service.
  • Where legally required (e.g., court order or law enforcement).

No international transfers outside the UK/EEA occur. All processing is performed in the UK.

8. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect personal data against unauthorised access, loss, or breach, in line with UK GDPR Article 32.

9. Your Rights under UK GDPR

You have the following rights (subject to applicable exemptions):

  • Access, rectification, erasure (“right to be forgotten”), restriction of processing.
  • Objection to processing (including legitimate-interest-based processing).
  • Not to be subject to solely automated decisions without safeguards (as detailed in Section 4).
  • Data portability (where applicable).
  • Lodge a complaint with the UK Information Commissioner’s Office (ICO) at https://ico.org.uk.

To exercise any right, contact the Developer (Section 12). We will respond within one month.

10. Children’s Privacy

The Service is not directed at children under 13. Discord’s minimum age is 13; we do not knowingly process data of children under 13. If you believe such data has been processed, contact us immediately for deletion.

11. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Policy to reflect changes in the Service or legal requirements. Material changes will be notified via the Bot or Discord where possible. Continued use constitutes acceptance.

12. Contact

For any privacy queries, data subject requests, or to exercise your rights, contact the Developer through the official support channel listed in the Bot’s documentation or server. Please provide your Discord User ID and relevant server details to enable a timely response.
